Welcome to RIT Group of Institutions

RIT Group of Institutions has been promoted by charitable institution 'ADITYA CHARITABLE TRUST' with the main objective of providing primary education, secondary education and higher education primarily in the field of Engineering, Dental, Medical, Science & Management etc. for needy students, intellectuals and other persons of the Society belonging to any caste, creed and religion, etc. especially for child and women development in rural and urban areas.
